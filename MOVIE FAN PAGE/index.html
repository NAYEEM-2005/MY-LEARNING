Character Bios

Prabhas as Salaar Devaratha Raisar

Salaar is a legendary warrior, driven by a fierce sense of justice and unyielding loyalty. His journey is one of vengeance and redemption, as he battles against formidable foes to protect those he loves.

Shruti Hassan as Aadhya

Aadhya is not just a love interest; she is a warrior in her own right. With her intellect and resilience, she supports Salaar in his quest, proving that strength comes in many forms.

Prithviraj Sukumaran as Varadha Rajamannar

Varadha is a cunning and ruthless antagonist, whose strategic mind poses a significant threat to Salaar. His motivations are complex, driven by ambition and a thirst for power.

Jagapathi Babu as Rajamannar

Rajamannar is the wise mentor, whose life experiences guide Salaar and Aadhya through their challenges. His insight and perspective are crucial in their battle against darkness.

Sriya Reddy as Radha Rama

Radha Rama serves as the emotional core of the story, embodying strength and compassion. Her unwavering support for Salaar and Aadhya helps them navigate their turbulent journey.
